## Image Requirements

- **Format:** JPG, PNG, or WebP
- **Recommended Size:** 400x400 pixels (square, 1:1 aspect ratio)
- **Min Size:** 300x300 pixels
- **Max File Size:** 300KB per image
- **Quality:** High quality, professional photos
- **Style:** Professional headshots or portrait photos
- **Background:** Clean background preferred (will be displayed in circular frame)

## Display

- Images are displayed in circular frames
- Candidate photo: 200px diameter (desktop), 180px (tablet), 150px (mobile)
- Spouse photo: 180px diameter (desktop), 160px (tablet), 130px (mobile)
- Photos appear on the left, names on the right (side-by-side layout)
- On mobile, photos appear on top, names below

## How to Add

1. Place your candidate photo in this folder as `kaveri-talekar.jpg`
2. Place your spouse photo in this folder as `sachin-talekar.jpg`
3. Refresh your browser - photos will appear automatically!

## Image Optimization

Before uploading:
- Crop to square (1:1 ratio)
- Optimize using [TinyPNG.com](https://tinypng.com/)
- Ensure good lighting and clarity
- Professional appearance recommended

## Troubleshooting

### Photo Not Showing?
- Check filename matches exactly: `kaveri-talekar.jpg` and `sachin-talekar.jpg`
- Verify file is in `public/images/candidates/` folder
- Clear browser cache (Ctrl+F5)
- Check browser console for errors (F12)

### Photo Looks Distorted?
- Ensure image is square (1:1 aspect ratio)
- Recommended: 400x400 pixels minimum
- Use image editing software to crop to square

### Wrong Photo?
- Verify filename matches the person
- Check `src/data/candidateData.json` for image paths
